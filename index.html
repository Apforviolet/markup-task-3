In Moscow, Sakharov Avenue is a rally "Let go"

In support of political prisoners. According to the final data “White counter” (at 17:00), 25.2 thousand people participated in the rally. The action took place on Academician Sakharov Avenue from 15:00 to 17:00, it was organized by the Libertarian Party. Its participants advocated ending criminal case brought up after an unauthorized rally on July 27, “cases FBK "on money laundering and in support of" those who are being prosecuted for his civic and political activities. ” The action was attended, in particular, by politician Alexei Navalny, head of the FBK Ivan Zhdanov,  former defendants in the July 27 affair Daniil Konon, Vladislav Barabanov, Valery Kostenok and Alexey Minyailo, actor Alexander Pal.

The rally was led by the editor-in-chief of Mediazones Sergey Smirnov and the deputy chief editor Echo of Moscow radio station Tatyana Felgenhauer.
